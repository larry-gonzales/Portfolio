About me

Hello, my name is Larry Gonzales.


I was born in California, raised in Pennsylvania, and currently live in New Jersey. I am keen on biking, card and board games, college football, a good beer, and (arguably) have an unhealthy obsession with hockey.


I'm a Senior Graphic Designer currently shifting my career into full-time web development. Design has always been my passion, but, as these two worlds have gotten closer, I have found that building great content is just as rewarding as creating it.


Learning new languages, methodologies, and frameworks present no limit to what I can learn to grow and improve my craft. I am eager to build a strong future as a web developer by taking advantage of my creative talents and problem-solving abilities.
